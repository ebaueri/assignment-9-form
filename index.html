How well do you know Miami Marlins history?

This year's World Series between the Chicago Cubs and Cleveland Indians is one that pits two underdogs — Chicago's last title came in 1908, while Cleveland's was in 1948 — against each other. This year, one team's title drought will be over. But if not for the Marlins franchise, it's possible that both droughts would have ended a long time ago.

After all, it was the Marlins who beat Cleveland in seven games back in the 1997 World Series, and it was the Marlins who knocked the Cubs out of the playoffs in 2003. So while the Cubs and Indians will get all kinds of attention this year, it's the Marlins who ensured an eventual World Series for the ages by sewing disappointment into both Chicago and Cleveland during their own playoff runs. So in honor of those upsets, let's see how well you know Marlins history.
